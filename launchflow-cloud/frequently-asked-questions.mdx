title: Frequently Asked Questions
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When will LaunchFlow support [X]?">
    We have many more features planned and will be releasing new ones regularly. If you have a specific feature in mind that is blocking you or you would like to see it sooner please reach out in [slack](https://join.slack.com/t/launchflowusers/shared_invite/zt-280e6a5ck-zfCrKbqw5w89L~0Xl55G4w) or reach out to us directly at founders@launchflow.com
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does LaunchFlow store my Pulumi state?">
    LaunchFlow doesn't store Pulumi state. See our [BuildFlow documentation](https://docs.buildflow.dev/programming-guide/buildflow-yaml#pulumi-configure).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Something went wrong and I need help?">
    LaunchFlow founders are available anytime to help with any issues you run into. Join our [slack](https://join.slack.com/t/launchflowusers/shared_invite/zt-280e6a5ck-zfCrKbqw5w89L~0Xl55G4w) or reach out to us directly at founders@launchflow.com to get assistance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the difference between BuildFlow and LaunchFlow?">
    BuildFlow is a completely open source framework for building and deploying python systems. LaunchFlow is a managed cloud that allows you to easily deploy and managed running BuildFlow applications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
