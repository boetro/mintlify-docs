---
title: Subscription and Billing
---

Monthly you will be billed for your usage over the past month starting on the day you sign up. 
For example if you signed up on January 3rd, your first bill will be sent on February 3rd for your usage from January 3rd to February 3rd. 

To view all your previous invoices or update your payment method you can use our [billing dashboard](https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/cN2eYv6P38Ys4P6cMM). To access the dashboard simply provide the email that you used when subscribing.
